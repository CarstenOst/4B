Overview 4B

What we are good at

  1. Helping each other.

  2. Making it easy for one another.

  3. Including.

  4. Good working environment.

  5. Finding solutions to obstcales.

  6. Always letting the group know if you are unable to attend the group meeting or will be late.

  7. Even if it's difficult at the start we always make it to the end.

What we are bad at

  1. Get started with tasks effectively.

  2. Poor distribution of tasks.

  3. The communication between us is weak at times.

  4. The efficiency of doing the task is poor. We delay them until we have several tasks to do in the same week.

  5. Professors and mentors should be reached out to more frequently.

How we can develop as a team

  1. We should obviously work on everything at the "What we are bad at" box.

  2. Decide what we'll work on at the next group meeting the day before so we can read the assignment at home and be more prepared for the task.

  3. For a better overview of tasks, we should create a document that refers to the tasks to be completed this week, next week, and so on.
